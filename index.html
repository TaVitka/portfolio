About me

I’m a front-end developer from Ukraine, currently living in Stockholm and learning swedish at an SFI course. My journey into web development began with a career shift — I originally worked in journalism but became interested in how websites are created and how design turns into something functional. I decided to give it a try, and it quickly became my passion.

With over two years of experience, I’ve worked with WordPress and Magento 2, creating custom websites that seamlessly combine design and functionality. I specialize in HTML, CSS (Sass, Less), and JavaScript, using tools like Git, Docker, DDEV, Gulp, Parcel and other to build responsive, pixel-perfect web pages.

For me, web development is more than just writing code — it’s about bringing ideas to life. I love transforming designs into functional websites that not only look great but also help businesses grow and improve user experiences.
